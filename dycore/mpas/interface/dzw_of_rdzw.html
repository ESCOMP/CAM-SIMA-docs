private pure elemental function dzw_of_rdzw_real32(rdzw) result(dzw)

Compute the differences in between w-wind levels dzw from the reciprocal differences in between w-wind levels rdzw, where is the vertical coordinate, and w-wind levels are synonymous with layer interfaces in MPAS. (KCW, 2025-10-20)
